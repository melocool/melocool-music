Melocool Music

AI-Powered Music Generation Platform

Overview

Melocool Music is an AI-driven music creation platform that enables users to transform text or lyrics into complete, professional-quality songs.

Formerly known as LyricsToSongAI, Melocool Music is part of the Melocool AI creative ecosystem.

Core Capabilities

  • Text-to-music generation with vocals and instruments
  • Support for 50+ music genres, moods, and tempos
  • AI-assisted lyric writing
  • Vocal separation, stem isolation, audio enhancement
  • Export formats including WAV, MP3, and MIDI

How It Works

  1. Enter lyrics or a text prompt
  2. Select musical style and parameters
  3. Generate a complete AI-produced song
  4. Download and publish the audio output

Users & Use Cases

Songwriters, content creators, filmmakers, game developers, producers, and hobbyists can all use Melocool Music. Supports creators from beginners to professionals in 150+ countries.

Melocool Music

AI-Powered Music Generation Platform

Overview

Melocool Music is an AI-driven music creation platform that enables users to transform text or lyrics into complete, professional-quality songs. The platform generates vocals, melodies, harmonies, and instrumental arrangements without requiring musical or production experience.

Formerly known as LyricsToSongAI, Melocool Music represents the evolution of our AI music technology into a unified creative product under the Melocool AI ecosystem.

Core Capabilities

  • Text-to-music and lyrics-to-song generation
  • Support for 50+ music genres, moods, and tempos
  • AI-assisted lyric writing and song structure
  • Vocal separation, stem isolation, and audio enhancement
  • Export formats including WAV, MP3, and MIDI

How It Works

  1. Enter lyrics or a text prompt
  2. Select musical style and parameters
  3. Generate a complete AI-produced song
  4. Download and publish the audio output

Users & Use Cases

Melocool Music is used by songwriters, content creators, filmmakers, game developers, producers, and hobbyists. The platform supports creators from beginners to professionals across more than 150 countries.
